Where do I get the .p8?

  1. Go to developer.apple.com → Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles → Keys.
  2. Click +, name it (e.g. "ApexSight APNs"), tick Apple Push Notifications service (APNs), Continue → Register.
  3. Download the .p8 (you can only download it once) and note the Key ID.
  4. Find your Team ID under Membership.
  5. Upload the .p8 above and paste both IDs. Done.
